# KB Entry Template

Use this template as the starting point for any new ShopFloor knowledge base article.

## YAML frontmatter

```yaml
---
title: Short descriptive name
summary: One-line description for listings and search
category: tool | technique | component | safety | meta
status: draft | starter | reviewed
tags: [tag1, tag2]
---
```

## Article structure

### Title

A clear, plain-language heading.

### What it is

2–4 sentences describing the thing. Assume the reader has never seen it.

### What it is used for

Bullet list of common, realistic use cases. Avoid marketing language.

### Beginner-safe first use

Numbered steps. Start from zero. Mention prerequisites (tools, skills, safety gear). If a step requires prior experience, say so and suggest learning resources.

### Safety / stop conditions

Bullet list of hard stops, hazards, and when to call a professional. Every article must have this section, even if the answer is "none beyond normal workshop awareness."

### Related links

Wiki-style links to other KB articles: `[[Article Title]]`. Only link to articles that actually exist or are planned.

## Writing rules

- Use plain language. No jargon without definition.
- Be cautious. Use "may," "can," and "usually" instead of absolute claims.
- Never give step-by-step instructions for dangerous work (mains voltage, battery puncture, chemical handling) unless the article is explicitly marked expert-only and includes full safety context.
- Include a "when to stop" cue in every procedure.
- Prefer "consider a professional repair" over overconfident DIY encouragement.
