# Stop conditions

Stop conditions are the moments when continuing is more likely to cause harm than help.

ShopFloor should make it normal to stop. Stopping is not failure. Sometimes stopping is the most useful thing a person can do.

## Common stop conditions

- Battery swelling, heat, smoke, or burnt smell
- Sparks, popping sounds, or visible electrical damage
- Liquid inside a device
- Important data with no backup
- A person asks you not to continue
- You do not understand the next step
- A repair needs tools or experience you do not have
- The work could create legal, medical, housing, or safety consequences

## What to do when you stop

1. Make the situation safe.
2. Tell the person clearly what you observed.
3. Do not guess beyond what you know.
4. Suggest the next safer path.
5. Leave a field note if it helps the next person and does not expose private details.
